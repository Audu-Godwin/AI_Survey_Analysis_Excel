This project analyzes Artificial Intelligence (AI) survey, a survey conducted  using Google Sheet and Microsoft Excel. The analysis involved data collection, data cleaning, pivot table analysis, dashboard creation, and generating insights on AI usage patterns and perceptions.

## Key Insights

- The survey collected responses from 40 participants and 38 left after data cleaning, providing insights into AI usage and perception.

- Male respondents represented the majority of participants (79%), while female respondents accounted for 21%.

- The largest age group among respondents was above 35 years, with 26 participants.

- ChatGPT was the most widely used AI tool, with 29 users indicating strong adoption.

- Daily AI usage was the highest usage frequency category (42%), showing regular interaction with AI tools.

- Most respondents agreed that AI improves learning experiences and supports productivity.

- 92% of respondents indicated they would recommend AI tools to other students, showing a positive perception of AI adoption.

## Conclusion

The analysis shows that AI tools are widely accepted and viewed as useful learning support tools.

The findings highlight strong AI adoption, particularly through tools such as ChatGPT, and demonstrate the growing role of AI in improving learning experiences.
